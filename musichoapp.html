Musicho App

INTRODUCTION

Studies have shown that music is one of the ways to treat illness, it is beneficial for both mental and physical diseases. For the general public, music is also easier to express mood than words and pictures. It is easier to resonate with other people without conceiving words or composition. On this platform, you are able to share or promote your own music. With the popular use of social media to share our lives, it gathers all kinds of users who are both content providers and content receivers. It is also a channel to promote and publicize musical works. In this music-friendly environment, more users will come into contact with different types of music, and expand discussions.
